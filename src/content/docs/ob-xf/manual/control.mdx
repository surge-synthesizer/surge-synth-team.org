The Control section contains a few options that affect how the synthesizer responds to incoming MIDI data.

## Pitch Bend

- **Down** and **Up**: These  number fields set the range of the pitch bend wheel in semitones, from 0 to 48. 
- **OSC 2 Only**: makes the pitch bend affect only Oscillator 2.

## Vibrato

The Vibrato settings include a dedicated LFO that modulates the pitch of both oscillators. Its amplitude is modulated by the Mod Wheel.
- **Rate**: Sets the speed of the vibrato LFO.
- **Shape**: Switches between a triangle and a square
