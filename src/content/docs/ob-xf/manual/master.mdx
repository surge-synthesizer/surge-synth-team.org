# Master

The Master section simply contains 3 parameters:
- **Volume**: Controls the output level of the synthesizer.
- **Transpose**: Transposes the pitch of all notes played by the synthesizer up or down from -24 to +24 semitones.
- **Tune**: Fine-tunes the pitch of all notes played by the synthesizer from -100 to +100 cents.
