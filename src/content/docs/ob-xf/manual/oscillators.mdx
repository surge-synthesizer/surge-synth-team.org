# Oscillators

The Oscillators section contains 2 independent oscillators, each with its own waveform, pitch, and modulation options.


## Tuning Controls

Each oscillator has its own tuning controls:
- **OSC1**: Controls the coarse tuning of Oscillator 1, from -24.00 to 24.00 semitones.
- **Detune**: Controls the fine tuning of Oscillator 2, from 0.1 to 100 cents. Like the OB-X, the two oscillators will never be perfectly in tune with each other, thus why this control won't go down to 0 cents, creating a naturally warmer sound.
- **OSC2**: Controls the coarse tuning of Oscillator 2, from -24.00 to 24.00 semitones.


## Waveform Selectors
Each oscillator has its own waveform selector buttons. On the left is the **sawtooth** wave, and on the right is the **pulse** wave.
If neither of these buttons is selected, the oscillator outputs a **triangle** wave, and the triangle wave icon between the two buttons is highlighted.

## Pulse Width and Offset

- **PW**: Controls the pulse width of both oscillators. It is applicable if either oscillator is set to a pulse wave. It ranges from 50% to 97.5%.
- **OSC 2 Offset**: Additionally offsets the pulse wave of Oscillator 2 relative to Oscillator 1. It ranges from 50% to 97.5%.

## Crossmod and Sync

- **Crossmod**: Adjusts the amount of frequency modulation applied from Oscillator 1 to Oscillator 2, ranging from 0% to 100%.
- **Sync**: Enables hard synchronization of Oscillator 2 to Oscillator 1. Effectively, the waveform of Oscillator 2 is restarted every time Oscillator 1 is itself restarted (waveform begins a new cycle), creating a more complex waveform.

## Bright

Increasing the **Bright** control adds en emphasis on high-frequency harmonics to the output of both oscillators, ranging from 0% to 100%. This can help add presence and clarity to the sound.

## Env to Pitch, Env to PW

- **Env to Pitch**: Adjust the amount of modulation applied from the Filter Envelope to the pitch of Oscillator 2 only (if **OSC 1+2** is disabled). It ranges from 0.00 to 36.00 semitones.
- **Env to PW**: Adjust the amount of modulation applied from the Filter Envelope to the pulse width of Oscillator 2 only (if **OSC 1+2** is disabled).

## Inv and OSC 1+2

- ** Inv**: Inverts the polarity of the **Env to Pitch** and **Env to PW** modulations.
- **OSC 1+2**: Enables each modulation source to affect both oscillators simultaneously.
